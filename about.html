About Me

A little bit about my personality, passions, and what drives me.

Hello, I'm Marselina!

I'm a student at SMK Negeri 1 Bawang, currently in the 11th grade, focusing on Software Engineering (RPL). I have a huge passion for visual creativity, which I explore by designing in Canva. I love the process of taking simple elements and arranging them into something beautiful and meaningful.

My journey into the digital world also led me to UI design, where I had the chance to create a concept for the Serulingmas Zoo ticket booking application. While my career path shifted from wanting to be a train driver, my goal remains the same: to become a successful person in a field I'm passionate about. I believe technology and design are the perfect way for me to achieve that.
